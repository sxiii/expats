Roll into the excitement every Thursday at our Weekly Board Game Night! Join fellow enthusiasts for a recurring evening of strategic gameplay, friendly competition, and a welcoming community of gamers. Whether you're a seasoned player or a novice, our Thursday nights are dedicated to laughter, camaraderie, and discovering new board game favorites. Bring your competitive spirit and join us as we make each Thursday memorable with a fresh array of tabletop adventures. Feel free to bring your own board games, adding a personal touch to our game night. Food & Drinks

Drinks will be available on site for purchase. Food is available in Mathallen. You are allowed to bring your food to HoN facility.

Being late and getting help

If you can't come at the beginning of the event, that's totally OK. You can basically come any time you like (during event hours). We will try to arrange a play group for you at all times. If you need help with finding a game or spot, ask me (Den).
